Introduction
1.1 Introduction to Topic
When moving or experiencing an emergency, vehicle owners have serious difficulties to find qualified
mechanics who are also honest enough and willing to work on the vehicle in case of a breakdown or
during regular maintenance. About 65 percent of the consumers complain of insecurities on trust and
reliability in search of mechanics (jmc equipment , 2022). The owners of vehicles lose a lot of time
visiting a variety of repair services only to find out that the necessary specialists are not available, the
service is too far, or the service cannot provide knowledge about this brand. According to industry
researches, more than 70 percent of clients are anxious of leaving their cars in unfamiliar repair shops
without reviews and certifications leaving them vulnerable to receiving poor quality services and
which cost them money.
Competent mechanics, especially solitary workers, find it just as difficult to find a way to the potential
clients who are not in their direct vicinity and gain their legitimacy on the market. The old method of
word-of-mouth marketing and making of bookings through telephone do not work effectively, and
this restricts opportunities of expanding business and complexity of building of trust in service
relationship (jmc equipment , 2022).
Mechanic Finder + Live ETA and Booking is a solution to this vacuum that will provide a transparent
ecosystem as between vehicle owners and certified mechanics in real-time. With the help of modern
web technologies, the platform allows its users to form full-fledged profiles with the models of
vehicles, the locations of their favorite services, and which requirements they prefer. Mechanics are
able to confirm certifications, specialization, area of services and expertise. The platform uses smart
database and correlates mechanics against vehicle owners depending on location distance, brand focus,
real time services, customer rating and service record.
The platform will have live tracking and approximate arrival time making it dynamic and therefore
creating trust and efficiency. This allows car owners to make a better choice and service requests to
be handled efficiently by the mechanics and turn the traditional automotive repair into the modern-

1.2 Problem Scenario
• The vehicle owners tend to have issues locating good people and even accessible mechanics in the
case of emergency or traveling to new locations due to which quite a considerable part of the stress
and unsafe conditions may be experienced.
• Mechanics lack alternative access to the potential consumers who are not in their local network
and have no channel to put their credentials, certifications, and areas of specialization in their
proper places.
• It is not simple to have vehicle owners check the qualification of the mechanics, the genuine
customer review and to ensure that the mechanic is specialized in the type of vehicle (bike or car)
he is handling which results into mismatched service expectations.
• Current solutions are usually rooted on traditional telephone based booking system which is not
efficient and can lead to a miscommunication, in addition, it does not record service history.
• On-site automotive service platforms available do not have any specifications of emergency onsite services provided, or they lack detailed mechanic profile, which is specific to vehicle brand.

to match vehicle owners to verified mechanics in real time effectively. It aims at streamlining

5 Methodology
5.1 Considered Methodology
5.1.1 RUP Methodology
Figure 1: Figure of RUP Methodology.
Rational Unified Process (RUP) is a management technique in software development best aligned with
large organizations that develop complex and large-scale systems which in turn use a huge team. It
organizes the project life cycle into four stages namely Inception, Elaboration, Construction, and
Transition. The first of these activities includes inception involving requirements gathering, feasibility,
and project scope and objectives. Refinement of requirements, dealing with major risks and the
establishment of a stable architectural bottom is realised during Elaboration. Construction majorly
involves implementation of the system that is achieved by codifying, testing, and integrating and the
Transition stage majorly requires delivery of the product, user training, and feedback assessment to
ensure that the user requirements are met (geeksforgeeks.org, 2024).

5.1.2 Waterfall Methodology
Figure 2: Figure of Waterfall Methodology.
Waterfall Model is a conventional, linear software development approach that matches to small or a
clear project whose requirements are constant. It breaks development in to stages sequentially:
Requirements, Analysis, Design, Implementation, Testing, Deployment and the Maintenance. During
the Requirement and Analysis stages, the needs of the stakeholders are collected, recorded and
assessed on technical, business and financial viability. Design stage generates specifications in
components and architectural specifications. These designs are then implemented into working code
then Testing is done to identify and fix the defects. And lastly, Deployment deploys the system to
users, and Maintenance addresses the post-release concerns and improvement with time
(BrowserStack, 2024).

5.1.3 Prototype Methodology
Figure 3: Figure of Prototype Methodology.
The Prototyping Model is a loose and dynamic software development model that would be adequate
in those projects that have ambiguous or may vary requirements and that the constant user feedback
is essential. It is normally carried out in the steps of Requirement Identification, Prototype
Development, User Evaluation, Refinement and Final System Development. The first requirements
are collected to create a working prototype that serves as a basis of the developed system,
implementing the main features enabling users to have contact with the real form of the system. User
Evaluation requirements are then converted into feedback during the Refinement stage where
functionality, usability and design can be rectified in successive phases until expectations are yielded
and the finished system is then engineered, tested and deployed using the tested prototype
(geeksforgeeks, 2023).

5.2 Selected Methodology
5.2.1 Agile Methodology
Figure 4: Figure of Agile Methodology.
Agile methodology can be described as an iterative, incremental software development methodology
that focuses on flexibility, collaboration and continuous improvement. It is based on the Agile
Manifesto, with its focus on the people and interactions, software, cooperation with customers, and
readiness to changes. Scrum, Kanban and Extreme Programming frameworks use these principles with
short development cycles and frequent feedback by stakeholders, allowing new elements to be added
to the team by responding fast to changing needs. Agile is considered to be more efficient in dealing
with uncertainty, developing more autonomy in the team, and developing more quality products, as
compared to the linear models, such as Waterfall, and therefore, the approach is considered more
customer-focused towards development in complicated changing environments. (Laoyan, 2025).

10 Conclusion
Mechanic Finder + Live ETA & Booking is a solution to one of the most urgent problems of the
automotive service market as it will create a clear, convenient application that will provide vehicle
owners with real-time access to qualified mechanics. This platform will change the access to and
delivery of the vehicle repair service with the help of the location-based search, live-tracking
system, the rich mechanic profiles, and the authenticated review system (Socket.io, 2024).
Based on the modern web technologies such as React, Node.js, MySQL, and Socket.io, relying on
the Agile SCRUM development approach, this project will provide a high-quality, user-friendly
platform that can offer the advantages of the vehicle owners who will need reliable help and the
professional mechanics who will need to gain more business opportunities. The solution simplifies
mechanic discovery in time of emergency but also creates a trust and accountability atmosphere
in the automotive service environment, showing it to be a viable solution (w3school, 2024).
The project is a great chance to use theoretical base in practice and act widely with the full-stack
web development, real-time communications system, and modern software engineering patterns.
The effective implementation of Mechanic Finder will be used to prove that technology-based
solutions can effectively be used to modernize traditional service industries (FullStack Labs,

Mechanic Finder:
I am writing to propose my final-year project titled “Mechanic Finder + Live ETA &
Booking.” This project is a smart web-based platform designed to connect vehicle owners with
verified nearby mechanics in real time. It addresses the common challenge of finding reliable and
available mechanics, especially in unfamiliar areas or emergency situations.
The system enables users to search for available mechanics based on their live location, view
their estimated time of arrival (ETA), and book either on-site assistance or scheduled repair
services. Mechanics can manage their availability, accept or reject booking requests, and
update their live location while traveling to the customer’s destination. This creates smooth,
transparent interaction between the user and the mechanic.
The platform also checks and certifies Mechanic for capabilities of repairing bikes & cars of every
kind, Mechanics have their own profile and on that they can add the brand of bike & cars they are
comfortable with repairing. And seeing that the vehicle owners can see the reviews and brands of
bike and cars the mechanic can repair and are comfortable with, the vehicle owners can decide to
go for the repair or call.
The platform is built using React for the front-end, Node.js for the backend, and MySQL as the
database. It incorporates real-time communication through Socket.io and map integration
using Leaflet or Google Maps API to display mechanic locations and live ETAs accurately.
In addition to core features, the system enhances user confidence and comfort by allowing
customers to view each mechanic’s professional profile, including:
• The brands and vehicle types (motorbikes or cars) the mechanic is certified or experienced
to repair.
• Customer reviews and ratings that reflect their service quality and reliability.
• A visual section showing the number of customers who have previously booked services
from that mechanic.
This added layer of transparency helps users make informed decisions and feel more comfortable
entrusting their vehicles to verified professionals rather than unknown local shops.
Overall, Mechanic Finder + Live ETA & Booking aims to simplify vehicle repair assistance,
improve trust between customers and service providers, and bring convenience to both parties
through the use of modern web technologies. The platform not only streamlines the process of
finding and booking mechanics but also contributes to building a more connected and reliable
vehicle service ecosystem.
